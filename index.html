Merlin's Manor

A solo-developed round-based survival horror game. Each round, a Minion is selected with the objective of eliminating all the other players. Players must survive the round by maneuvering through a map consisting of a 7x7 grid of rooms and avoiding the Minion. The Minion can equip upgrades that grant them abilities to assist in eliminating. The game ends when the Minion has eliminated everyone or the players have survived the entire duration of the round.
(Game playable on the Roblox platform. Coded in Lua using the Roblox Studio environment)
