No Splotch Gallery

Welcome to No Splotch Gallery! In this art-themed puzzle platformer, arm yourself with a paint gun and set out to restore the missing splotches inside the paintings. Use the different colored bullets to apply special properties to platforms, solve the puzzles, and make your way to each splotch. Will you be the one who restores the gallery back to its former glory?

Created in Unity in a team of 4 over 6 weeks.
Won 5th place at the Winter 2022 UM + EMU Game Development Showcase!

Merlin's Manor

A solo-developed round-based survival horror game. In each round, get chosen as the Minion and eliminate all players, or get chosen as a player and survive until the end.

Players: Navigate a 7x7 grid of rooms to avoid the Minion and survive.
Minion: Use upgrades to unlock new abilities and eliminate all players.

Created in Roblox Studio.

Morse Decode

A Morse Code puzzle game catered towards the visually impaired.
In each level, listen closely and decode the given Morse Code message in order to win.

A Fall 2019 semester-long project in Engineering 100: Gaming for the Greater Good at the University of Michigan.
Created in a team of 4 using Gamemaker.
